Oven Baked Fries

Description:

Crispy oven baked fries are so easy and delicious served with creamy fry sauce. The perfect side dish or gluten-free snack.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large potatoes sliced into fries
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp salt
  • For the fry sauce:
    • ½ cup mayonnaise
    • 2-3 tbsp ketchup
    • 1 tsp Tabasco (optional)
    • 2-3 tsp pickle juice (add a teaspoon at a time, taste and adjust)

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 200ºC/390ºF.
  2. Toss the sliced potatoes with the oil and salt.
  3. Spread the fries onto a large baking pan/sheet in a single layer and place into the oven.
  4. Allow to bake for 20-30 minutes then flip and continue to bake for another 20-30 minutes or until the fries are golden brown and crisp all around.
  5. In the meantime, combine all the ingredients for the fry sauce and mix well.
  6. When the fries are baked, remove from the oven and serve with the fry sauce.
